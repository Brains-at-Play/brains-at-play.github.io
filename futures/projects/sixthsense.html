Fall 2020

The 6th Sense

A Speculative Game Design Workshop

Version One

September 14th, 2020

A short and fast-paced workshop about creating a digital game employing futuristic brain-to-brain interaction (BBI) technologies. The workshop starts with a brief introduction to speculative design and to the current status and envisioned capabilities of BBI technologies. Subsequently, through a series of engaging and fun series of design exercises, participants are guided towards conceptualizing, designing and playtesting a BBI game.


Version Two

October 2020 - March 2021

After the first session, Grammenos was invited by Marientina Gotsis and Dong Song to reimagine The 6th Sense for university students at USC—as well as primary school teachers and students in Greece.

Three complementary online events have been planned in Greece which will be held under the auspices of the Greek UNESCO Research Chair in Futures Research

Onboarding

October 26th, 2020

Aimed at Primary and Secondary Education Informatics Educators to get them onboarded/interested in participating with their schools in the 18 December version (see below), as well as to get constructive feedback. A case study will take place using observation methods, questionnaires and a focus group.

School Session

December 18th, 2020

Aimed at Primary and Secondary Education students (ages 10+). It will take place concurrently at several schools. At each school the Informatics teacher will facilitate several groups of students.

Public Session

December 19th, 2020

Aimed at and openly streamed for the general public.

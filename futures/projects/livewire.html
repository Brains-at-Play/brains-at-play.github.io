Livewire

March 5th, 2021

The Event

Livewire is a virtual entertainment event that combines neuroscience, neuroethics, and interactive media for participatory technology design.

As part of the USC Visions and Voices event series, Livewire will begin with keynotes on participatory neurotechnology design from USCâ€™s Dong Song as well as neuroethicists from the University of Washington Center for Neurotechnology and Neuroethics Canada. A web app for enabling multi-brain experiences using EEG headsets (provided by OpenBCI) is being developed by Mousai Neurotechnologies. A theatrical demonstration of this technology will serve as the focal point of small breakout discussions on the ethical, legal, and social implications of commercial brain devices.

Goals

  • Thought leadership
  • Creative showcase
  • Public engagement and participation
  • Community-building

Credits

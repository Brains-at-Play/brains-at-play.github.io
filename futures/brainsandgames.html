Brains and Games

International Design Fiction Competition

Welcome Gamers!

Brains and Games is an international design fiction competition for presenting imaginative ideas for brain-responsive multiplayer experiences.

International Competition

February 1st, 2021 — An introductory workshop hosted by Dimitris Grammenos and Garrett Flynn

March 10th, 2021 — Submission deadline

March 20th, 2021 — Participants share their submissions and winners will be announced during a virtual event for Brain Awareness Week

May-August, 2021 — Competition winners build working prototypes of their game with support from Brains@Play

Prize Categories

  1. General (sponsored by USC Games)
  2. VR + Neurotech + Health (sponsored by the USC SMART-VR Center)
  3. Web-Based Generative Art (sponsored by USC MA+P)

Greek Student Competition

December 18th - 19th, 2020 — Introductory workshops hosted by Dimitris Grammenos

March 10th, 2021 — Submission deadline

April 13th-18th, 2021 — Participants will present their submissions online at the Computing at School Festival

April 18th, 2021 — Winners announced

Prize Categories

  1. Primary School
  2. Gymnasium
  3. Lyceum

Submission Requirements

  1. Brains and Games Design Document (to be released)
  2. A 1920x1080 cover image (including the name of the game)
  3. A video, up to 3’

Note: Submissions to the Greek competition will be managed by the organizers of the Computing at School Festival

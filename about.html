About Us

Mission

Our mission at Brains@Play is to get more people interested in brain-computer interface (BCI) technologies while having fun!

Methods

To do this, we provide playful educational resources for students and educators to explore the implications of multi-brain experiences with an eye towards ethics as well as entertainment. These resources allow anyone, regardless of background, to design and create brain-responsive experiences.

Brains and Games

International Design Fiction Competition

Welcome Gamers!

We are hosting Brains and Games, an international design fiction competition where anyone can present their idea for a compelling multi-brain experience.

Competition Divisions

Greek Competition: For students of all ages. Entries in Greek. Prizes per age group.

International Competition: People of all ages. Entries in English. Prizes for top submissions.

Workshops

December 18th

Educators and teams of students at school.

December 19th

Teams of students at school or children at home.

The last part of this workshop will include a brief design task to come up with and present a game name and brief description using The Game from the Future prompt generator

Submission Deadline

We are accepting submissions for Brains and Games from January 1st to March 10th, 2021.

During Brain Awareness Week on March 20th, all participants will gather together to share their creations and receive their prizes!

Prizes

All winners will be invited to bring their games to life with a working prototype using OpenBCI EEG headsets.
